"A household rooftop solar panel system can reduce pollution by 100 tons of CO2 carbon dioxide in its lifetime—and this includes the energy it took to manufacture the solar panels..

When the sun isn’t shining, most modern solar units use net metering (a system hooked up to your city’s power grid that measures the difference between the energy you give back to the grid and the energy you use from the grid).

If you generate more solar energy than you’re consuming, it gets fed back to the grid and distributed elsewhere. Think about it, your solar energy could be helping power a school, hospital or your next-door neighbor.
